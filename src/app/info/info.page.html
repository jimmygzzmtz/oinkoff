Info Version 1.0 How to play Goal

The goal of the game is to be the player that passes the winnings score.

  • You gain or lose points depending on the way the pigs land.
  • The way the pigs land is random.
    •      How to play Rules

    Each player gets to throw the pigs each round.

  • The player's turn lasts until the player gets a 0 as a score or passes.
  • Passing saves the score to the player's total score.
    •      How to play Tips

    Strategy is the key to winning.

  • Plan on when to risk a higher score and when to save what you have.
  • It is always possible to get a 0 and lose what you have so far.
